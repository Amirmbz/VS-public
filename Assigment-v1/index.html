Summer Semester

The summer semester offers a unique opportunity for students to accelerate their academic progress, catch up on missed courses, or explore new areas of interest. Unlike the traditional fall and spring semesters, the summer term is typically shorter, often spanning just eight to twelve weeks. This condensed schedule allows students to complete a full course load in a fraction of the time, making it an ideal choice for those looking to graduate early or lighten their regular semester workload.
